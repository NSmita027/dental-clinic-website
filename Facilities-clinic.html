Facilities At The Clinic

The state of the art facilities at Kadali's Dental include:

  • Free pick up and wifi.
  • Parking available.
  • Accomodation facility for our esteemed patients.
  • Dental chairs are computerized, fully automatic and state of the art.
  • Intraoral cameras for image recording.
  • Intraoral xray machine.
  • RVG With minimum radiation and Instant X-ray images.
  • Zoom Advanced Power Authorized tooth whitening centre.
  • Light curing units and ultra fast lights for bonding and filling of teeth.
  • Teeth scaling and cleaning using ultrasonic scalers.
  • Root canal treatments done using rotary endodontic systems.
  • Nobel Biocare authorized centre for dental implants.
  • Fiber optic handpieces for accurate and precise work.
  • Total sterilization and disinfection using latest autoclaves.
  • Air abrasion unit for quick and effective stain removal.
  • All dental imaging, patient record management and patient education done by software technology.

    • Benefits At Kadali's Dental

    For your teeth

    Whichever dental treatment is required, we offer the most advanced and latest techniques and longest lasting dental materials for the same. We want your own healthy and white teeth to last you for your lifetime and will aim towards educating you about proper dental care and maintainance.

    For your comfort

    We understand that most people are uneasy about going to the dentist. Hence your ease and comfort is our utmost priority. Our working operatories provide complete privacy as they are individually located in separate rooms. Complimentary wi-fi, soft and relaxing music, complimentary beverages and foot massagers are amongst our various offerings to make you feel at home.

    For your budget

    Once the initial check up consultations are done, you will be informed in advance of all treatment options, costs, time line etc in detail. The actual treatment is started only after you are comfortable with the entire treatment details. You will be pleased to find us most cost effective.

    For your safety

    Our clinic in Mumbai has the most latest and advanced armamentarium including autoclaves, sterilizers and all disposables, where required. Thus both staff and patient safety is taken care off. Our team actively participates in cross infection control courses and is fully immunized and vaccinated. Please do not hesitate to ask if you need further information.
