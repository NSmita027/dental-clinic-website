Gum Treatment

Gum treatment involves any and all procedures concerning the gums either for a smile correction or for gum disease (periodontal disease).

Gum treatment for smile correction focuses on enhancing the appearance of the smile by addressing gum-related issues.

It involves procedures such as:

  1. Gum Contouring or Reshaping gums to improve gum symmetry and visibility.
  2. Gum lift to expose more tooth structure.
  3. Gum Grafting to cover exposed roots and treat gum recession.
  4. Laser Gum Treatment for Minimally invasive procedure using laser technology.

Gum treatment also called as periodontal treatment is necessary when gums are affected by disease or infection.

Types of gum treatment include:

  1. Scaling and Root Planing - deep cleaning to remove plaque and tartar.
  2. Gum Surgery- Procedures like flap surgery or bone grafting for advanced cases.
  3. Laser Gum Therapy - Minimally invasive treatment using laser technology.
