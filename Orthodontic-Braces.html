Orthodontic Braces

Orthodontic braces are dental appliances used to correct misaligned teeth, overcrowding and bite issues. They consist of brackets, wires and sometimes additional components like elastics.

Types of Orthodontic Braces

Traditional Metal Braces

Made of stainless steel, these are the most common type.

Ceramic Braces

Clear or tooth-colored brackets for a more discreet appearance.

Lingual Braces

Brackets attached to the back of teeth, invisible from the front.

Invisalign

Clear, removable aligners for mild to moderate orthodontic cases.

Benefits of Orthodontic Braces

1. Improved Oral Health: Straight teeth are easier to clean, reducing the risk of decay and gum disease.

2. Enhanced Aesthetics: A beautiful smile can boost confidence and self-esteem.

3. Better Bite Alignment: Proper bite alignment can improve chewing and reduce wear on teeth.

Teeth Aligners and Invisalign

Teeth aligners are custom-made, removable orthodontic devices used to straighten teeth and improve dental alignment. They are a popular alternative to traditional braces.

Benefits of Teeth Aligners

1. Aesthetics: Aligners are nearly invisible, making them a discreet option.

2. Removability: Aligners can be removed for eating, brushing, and special occasions.

3. Comfort: Aligners are often more comfortable than traditional braces.

4. Convenience: Fewer orthodontist visits are typically required.

Teeth aligners offer a convenient and aesthetically pleasing solution for achieving a straighter smile.
