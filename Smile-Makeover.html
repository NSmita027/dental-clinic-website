Smile Makeover

Smile Makeovers in India (Mumbai)

Smile makeover is that process where dental procedures like porcelain veneers, all ceramic crowns,teeth recontouring, gum contouring, tooth whitening, gum de-pigmentation are performed on several front teeth in order to produce an overall change in the appearance of your smile.

Smile makeovers begin with a smile design consultation where diagnostic data is gathered and your goals and expectations are discussed. A plan is then formulated to achieve your desired result in harmony with your other facial features and the image you want to project. A beautiful smile conveys self-confidence, warmth and approachability, traits that are important for both business and personal communications. We understand this, which is why we offer an extensive range of smile makeover procedures and products to suit your needs.
