Dental Tourism Mumbai, India

Looking for a break during the dental treatment? Or some sightseeing after the treatment?

Kadali's Dental clinic with their travel partners are pleased to offer you a wide array of choices for your dental travel. Just call Kadali's Corporate Travel Manager Shalu on email will provide to arrange flights, airport transfers, Car Hire, Accommodation etc. If you want, we can customize an entire package for you which includes the dental treatment costs and all your travel and Dental Tourism expenses.

Why Mumbai, India is Popular for Dental Vacations?

Dental tourism in Mumbai, India has been popular for many years now, particularly with Americans and Australians. Mumbai is very close and well connected to many exotic and exciting Indian locations like the back waters of Kerala, royal palaces of Rajasthan, Taj Mahal at Agra, endless beaches of Goa and many more. Plus travelling and stay is relatively inexpensive.

Alternatively, you can simply stay in Mumbai, and we can arrange day trips to exciting destinations in and around Mumbai.
